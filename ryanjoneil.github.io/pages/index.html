Hi, I'm Ryan.

I'm an operations research analyst and coder by day, Ph.D. student in optimization by night, blogger, amateur musician, and cocktail order consultant.

In my work, I guide and develop Zoomer's advanced mathematical capabilities for data analysis, business modeling, and optimization. Our combined Decision Engineering team builds algorithmic decision making components which integrate directly into real-time Logistics-as-a-Service (LaaS) systems.

My academic research applies mathematical optimization techniques to dynamic pickup and delivery problems under the direction of my advisor, Dr. Karla Hoffman. I'm also interested in cutting and packing problems, scheduling, modeling languages, and computational optimization.
